About Sangatan

Dangisaran Yuva Sangatan ek yuva sangathan ho jasko uddeshya yuva harulai ekjut garera samajik pariwartan, sahayog ra sakaratmak soch ko bikash garna ho.

Our Mission

  • Yuva haru lai ekjut banaune
  • Samajik seva ra sahayog
  • Naya soch ra pariwartan
  • Yuva ko pahichan ra bikash

Core Members

President[Arjun oli] , Vice President, Secretary[deepak oli], Treasurer ra anya members haru sangathan ko mukhya bhag hun.
